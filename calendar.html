02. December

For today I have a secret christmas recipe:

  • JvR's Mom's Cookies

  • 150g Butter
  • 100g Sugar
  • 180g Flour
  • 100g Brown Sugar
  • 1 TS Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Package Baking Powder
  • 100g Chopped Almonds
  • 150g Chocolate Drops

Mix butter with sugar and stir until creamy. Add vanilla first and then baking powder + flour. In the end add almonds and chocolate.
Pile up with a teaspoon on a baking sheet and bake 12 - 14 min on 175 degrees until gold brown.

03. December

It's the 3rd of December and as always I feel like time flies during these last months. Therefore I feel like it's important to consciously take a step back and take it a little slower some days. A great things to start one of those days is to refelct on the week that is almost over by now. Maybe you want to join me and answer these questions for yourself.

  • What are 3 things that made me happy this week?
  • Is there anybody that made this week special?
  • What am I looking forward to for the coming weekend?


Maybe this helps you to calm down, appreciate yourself and value the people most important to you.
